Team members

Our team members are composed of astronomers, physicists and engineers working side-by-side to undersand the astronomical events in the radio and optical wavelengths. Our scientific interests are radio and optical transients, including Fast Radio Bursts (FRBs). In addition, we are particularly interested in designing and building dedicated instrument to observe the transient space.
